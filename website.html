About LifeLink

Our Mission

To create a seamless connection between blood donors and recipients, ensuring timely access to safe blood donation services. We strive to make blood donation more accessible and efficient through technology.

Our Vision

To build a world where no life is lost due to lack of blood availability. We envision a community where blood donation is a regular practice and every hospital has adequate blood supply.
